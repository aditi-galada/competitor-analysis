Real Time Competitor Analysis

The website performs competitor analysis based on the latest product offerings of various brands. By using real time data, the problem of redundancy is eliminated and output remain relvant. The product offerings of the brands have been divided into three product categories t-shirts, shirts and jeans. Individual pages of each product category compare the offerings of the brands in terms of price points, techniques used, fits, styles and so on.
